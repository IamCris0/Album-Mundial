About the 2026 Panini album

The official Panini sticker album for the 2026 FIFA World Cup contains 980 stickers covering all 32 qualified nations, the FIFA tournament history, the official emblem, mascot, and host-city stadiums. It's the largest Panini World Cup album to date — the 2026 tournament is also the first played across three host countries (United States, Mexico, Canada) with an expanded 48-team field.

Each team gets 20 stickers: a team logo (number 1), a team photo (number 13), and 18 player portraits. Special foil stickers — including the official emblem, the mascot, and the trophy — are scattered throughout.

Scanini reads the code from the back of each sticker (e.g. ARG 10) using your phone camera, validates it against this 980-card checklist, and tracks your collection automatically — no manual data entry, no spreadsheets.
